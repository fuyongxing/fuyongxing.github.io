Biosketch

Fuyong Xing is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Biostatistics and Informatics at Colorado School of Public Health, University of Colorado Denver | Anschutz Medical Campus. He received his Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Florida, M.S. from Rutgers University-New Brunswick and bachelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University. His research focuses on medical image computing, imaging informatics, and machine learning.


He is looking for students who are interested in medical image analysis. Please feel free to contact him if you are interested. Students from the Department of Biostatistics and Informatics as well as other departments (e.g., Computer Science and Engineering, Bioengineering, etc.) are always welcome.
