Biosketch

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Biostatistics and Informatics at Colorado School of Public Health, University of Colorado Denver. I received my Ph.D. degree in the Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Florida. I received my bachelor and master degrees from Xi'an Jiaotong University and Rutgers University-New Brunswick, respectively. My research interests include medical image computing, imaging informatics, and machine learning. I also work on high performance computing for biomedical imaging informatics.
